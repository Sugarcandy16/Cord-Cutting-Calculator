Got Questions?

Do I need satellite to stream AT&T TV NOW?

Nope. With just an Internet connection, AT&T TV NOW lets you watch your favorite live and on-demand shows, plus the top premium channels. No satellite or cable box required.

Is AT&T TV NOW compatible with my device?

AT&T TV NOW plays the top live and on-demand entertainment on tons of popular devices. Explore the full list of supported browsers & devices.

How does the free trial work?

If you’re a new customer, its simple – if you purchase the PLUS, MAX, or International packages, you’ll get 7 free days to stream as much AT&T TV NOW as you can handle!

What is True Cloud DVR BETA?

With cloud DVR, you can record live TV and play it back on your own time! Right off the bat, you’ll get 20 hours of free storage. So now you can fast forward, rewind, and skip through commercials all on your favorite devices including:

  • Apple devices (iOS 10+) including Apple TVs
  • Android devices
  • Amazon Fire TV devices
  • Roku devices
  • Chromecast devices and TVs
  • Google Chrome and Safari browsers

Functions may be limited in beta. Compatible device required. Not available for select channels. Data connection required. Recordings expire after 30 days. Restrictions apply.

Can I watch on multiple devices at the same time?

With every AT&T TV NOW account, you’re able to stream on two devices at the same time. Need more? Upgrade to a third stream for $5/mo. and watch on up to three devices simultaneously.

What if I want to cancel?

Need a break from the entertainment (a TV-sabbatical, if you will)—no worries! With AT&T TV NOW™ we’ll never lock you into an annual contract, and you can cancel your service at any time. No questions asked.

Do you have on-demand titles?

Sure do! Live TV is just the beginning. With AT&T TV NOW you’ll also get access to thousands of on demand movies and shows – already included and playing on your schedule.

What if I have a promo code?

If you scored yourself a promo code, redeeming it is easy. Just create your account and enter your code at checkout.

Can I watch sports and local news in my area?

Stream your home team and local networks with AT&T TV NOW! Type your billing ZIP code in our handy look-up tool to see all the locals and Regional Sports Networks you’ll have access to.

Does AT&T TV NOW have international channels?

You bet! We’ve got packages from Brazil, Korea and Vietnam packed with hit shows, live sports and breaking news. Snag any international package on its own, group them together, or add as many as you want to a base package.

CAN I GET SPANISH CHANNELS?

Get 5 sports networks with Deportes for $5/mo. or 50+ channels of sports, news, movies and telenovelas with Español for $15/mo. See the channels

Still have questions? We’ve got answers!

Check out our Help Center or if you’re ready to stream, start your free trial and get ready to stream the dream!
